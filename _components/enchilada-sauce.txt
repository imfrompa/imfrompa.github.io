ingredients:
- 2 Tbls olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, fine chopped
- 6 Tbls Chili Powder
- 4 tsp coriander
- 4 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- 30oz box of tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup water

directions:
- Saute onion in oil until slightly brown
- Add garlic and spices to pan, cook and stir for 30 seconds.
- Add tomato sauce and water. 
- Bring to a simmer for 8 minutes
- Stir occasionally.
